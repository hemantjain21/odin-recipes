Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes in long sticks
  2. Half Fry them in oil
  3. Take them out and let them cool at room temperature
  4. Freeze them
  5. Now let them sit outside fridge until they come back to room temperature
  6. Fry them again
  7. Put them in a bowl and sprinkle salt to taste
