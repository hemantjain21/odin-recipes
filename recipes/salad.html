Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Beetroot
  • Paneer
  • Mayonaise
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Chop lettuce and beetroot in square pieces and open their sheets
  2. Add salt and black pepper to taste
  3. Add some olive oil
  4. Mix them and plate it in the serving bowl
  5. Squeeze some mayonaise on top for presentation
