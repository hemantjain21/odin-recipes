Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Oregano
  • Chilly Flakes
  • Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Maida
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Make white sauce by cooking maida in butter on low flame
  3. Pour milk and stir thoroughly so that no maida lumps remain in the sauce
  4. Add salt and black pepper as per the taste
  5. Add cheese
  6. Put boiled pasta in the sauce and reduce the sauce on medium flame to make a thick sauce
  7. Garnish with oregano, chilly flakes and olive oil and serve
